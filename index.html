Digital Collage Work

Links to Full-Size Images

Waves

This work combines three images: a seagull, an ocean wave, and a lantern. My aim was to experiment with abstraction techniques, by employing various software techniques and overlays.

Sunburst

This collage combines two nature images: a sunflower and a dandelion. My intent was to create an original visual of plants and natural forms.

Descent

This work combines two pictures: a lighthouse stairway and a cup of ice water. My intention was to complile non-related images to make them appear artistically similar.

Vortex

This piece combines a single paper crane and a glass sculpture. Reflections on other people's abstraction techniques inspired me to try a similar method.

Buzz

This project combines a few visuals from nature: a bee on a flower and a tree. My objective is to evoke an appreciation for nature's beauty.

Tahoe

I created this work, after a weekend in South Lake Tahoe. I used several images from that trip: sunglasses, seagulls, a mountain highway, and a pinecone. This collage is a visual representation of the Tahoe area's beauty.

Video Work

A Place Called Home

This experimental documentary captures one of my weekend trips to Sacramento. This visual narrative has significant meaning for me since I lived in Sacramento, prior to attending San Jose State University.

Springtime Composite

his video animates still objects- a lantern, a sunflower, and leaves; these visuals were compiled with urban sound recordings. The intent is to give the viewer a relaxed, Springtime feel.

Storm Symphony

This code project is personal for me since I remember having a childhood phobia of storms. I hope the sounds help others overcome similar weather-related phobias.

Night Insects

This piece is reflective of my two childhood memories: observing night insects and drawing butterflies. I made it so others could recreate similar memories.
