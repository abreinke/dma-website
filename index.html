Digital Collage Work

Links to Full-Size Images

Waves

This work combines various images: a seagull, a wave, and a lantern. I used a variety of effects and overlays to distort the images, as I wanted to experiment with abstraction techniques.

Sunburst

This collage combines two nature images: a sunflower and a dandelion. The reason I did this was to see what I could create by compiling plants and natural forms.

Descent

This work combines two pictures: a lighthouse stairway and a cup of ice water. I remember creating it since I wanted to complile non-related images and make them appear artistically similar.

Vortex

This piece combines various objects, such as a single paper crane and a glass sculpture. I created it since I had seen other people create abstracted images and became inspired to try a similar method.

Buzz

This project combines a few visuals from nature: a bee on a flower and a tree. I created it since I was inspired to combine images of natural forms.

Tahoe

I created this work, after a weekend in South Lake Tahoe. I used several images of things which I saw on that trip: sunglasses, seagulls, a mountain highway, and a pinecone. My intention of creating this collage was to merge together several pictures of things from the Tahoe area.

Artist Statement

Video Work

A Place Called Home

This experimental documentary captures one of my weekend trips to Sacramento. It's a piece which has significant meaning for me since I lived in Sacramento, before moving to San Jose for school.

Springtime Composite

I created this video by animating still objects- a lantern, a sunflower, and leaves- and compiling them with outdoor sounds. My objective was to give the viewer a relaxed, Springtime feel.

Storm Symphony

This code project is personal to me since I remember having a childhood phobia of storms. I hope the sounds help others overcome similar weather-related phobias.

Night Insects

This piece is reflective of my two childhood memories: observing night insects and drawing butterflies. I made it so others could recreate similar memories.
